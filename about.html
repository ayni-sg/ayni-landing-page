The Ayni Thesis

In a digital age defined by transactions, we are building a space defined by reciprocity. Ayni Social is an attempt to recover the "Kampung spirit"—the deep, local reliance we once had on our neighbors.

"We don't need more connections; we need better, more resilient ones."

🤝

Mutual Aid

Community resilience isn't found in markets, but in mutual aid. We believe that when we share our skills, tools, and time, we reduce our dependency on impersonal global supply chains.

🌱

The Kampung Spirit

The kampung was more than a village; it was a promise. Ayni Social brings that intimacy to your screen, making it easier to discover the people living just a floor or a street away.
