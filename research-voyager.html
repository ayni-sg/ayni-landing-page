FAQ

Questions that matter for thoughtful families and students.

Why does research matter?

Research develops the habits of inquiry, evidence, and reflection that are essential for university study and future careers.

Do I need prior research experience?

No. We welcome students at different stages, including those beginning their first structured research experience.

Is publication guaranteed?

No. Publication depends on the quality of the work and external review processes. Our focus is on authentic learning and mentorship.

How long does the programme take?

Most programmes run from eight to sixteen weeks depending on the pathway and student goals.

Who can join?

We support high school, junior college, polytechnic, undergraduate students, and families seeking a rigorous academic pathway.
